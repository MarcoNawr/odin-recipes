Recipe of Salad

Description

A light salad. Just the right thing for hot days.

But you can enjoy the salad at any time of year

Ingredients

  • 200 g Salad
  • 100 g Tomato
  • 100 g Cucumber
  • 1 Onion
  • 50 g Shepherd's cheese

Steps

  1. Clean all Ingredients
  2. Cut all Ingredients into small pieces
  3. Put all cutted Ingredients togehter
  4. Add salt and pepper to your preference
