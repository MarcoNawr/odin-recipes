Recipe of Spaghetti

Description

A Spaghetti recipe like in Italia. Mhhhhhh ahhhhhh ....

Nothing beats a good pasta and this Spaghetti will blow your mind! Enjoy

Ingredients

  • 500 g Spaghetti
  • 200 g Ketchupr
  • 20 Cloves of garlic
  • 1 Onion

Steps

Spaghetti

  1. boil the Spaghetti for 5-10 min. (salted water)
  2. When the Spaghetti is al dente, pour off the water

Souce

  1. Cut the Onion into small pieces
  2. Cut the Garlic into small pieces
  3. Roast in a frying pan
  4. Add the Ketchup
  5. Add salt and pepper to your preference
